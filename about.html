From Bold Vision to Continental Impact

In 2018, three frustrated software engineers in Lagos asked a simple question: "Why is world-class tech education only available to the privileged few?"

That question sparked a revolution. Starting with 15 students in a cramped co-working space, we've grown into Africa's most trusted tech education platform, but our mission remains the same: make exceptional tech education accessible to everyone.

Today, every 48 hours, another MDTH graduate starts their dream tech job. Our secret? We don't just teach code – we transform minds, build confidence, and create lasting change in communities across the continent.

2018 → 2024

From 15 students in Lagos to 15,000+ graduates across Africa. From one small room to partnerships with Google, Microsoft, and 200+ tech companies. From a dream to a movement that's reshaping an entire continent's tech landscape.

What Drives Us Every Day

Radical Accessibility

Excellence shouldn't be expensive. Through scholarships, flexible payments, and community programs, we've made world-class tech education available to street hawkers, farmers' children, and corporate executives alike.

Relentless Innovation

While others teach yesterday's skills, we're obsessed with tomorrow's opportunities. Our curriculum evolves monthly, guided by insights from Silicon Valley and shaped for African realities.

Community First

We're not just building careers – we're building a movement. Our 15,000+ strong alumni network spans from Cape Town to Cairo, creating opportunities and lifting each other up.

African Excellence

We're proudly African, solving African challenges with world-class solutions. Our graduates don't just get jobs – they start companies, lead teams, and shape the future of tech on the continent.
