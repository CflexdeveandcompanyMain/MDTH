Data Analytics Mastery Course

Master the art of transforming raw data into actionable insights with our comprehensive Data Analytics course. Learn industry-standard tools like Python, SQL, and Tableau while understanding the fundamental principles of data cleaning, visualization, and statistical analysis.

This course is designed for beginners and intermediate analysts who want to build a strong foundation in data analytics and create portfolio-worthy projects that will impress employers and clients in the African market.

What Will You Learn?

  • Completely understand the data analytics process from collection to visualization
  • How to clean and preprocess data for analysis
  • Master SQL for database querying and management
  • Python programming for data analysis with Pandas and NumPy
  • Create compelling data visualizations with Tableau and Power BI
  • Statistical analysis techniques for deriving insights
  • Build interactive dashboards for business intelligence
  • Present data findings effectively to stakeholders

Requirements

  • Basic computer literacy
  • A computer/Laptop (Mac or Windows)
  • Internet Connection
  • No prior programming experience required

Who is this course for?

  • Beginners - People who have little to no prior knowledge of data analytics
  • Business Professionals - Those who want to make data-driven decisions
  • Marketers - Professionals looking to analyze campaign performance
  • Students - Those preparing for careers in data-related fields
  • Entrepreneurs - Business owners who want to leverage data for growth

About the Instructor

Chinedu Adeyemi

Senior Data Analyst with 8+ years of experience

Chinedu is a seasoned data professional with extensive experience in transforming raw data into actionable insights for businesses across Africa. He has worked with major corporations including MTN, Dangote Group, and Flutterwave, helping them leverage data for strategic decision-making.

With a Master's degree in Data Science from the University of Lagos and multiple certifications in data analytics, Chinedu brings both academic knowledge and practical industry experience to his teaching. He is passionate about empowering African professionals with data skills that are relevant to our unique market context.

Chinedu has trained over 2,000 students across the continent, with many securing positions in top companies or using their new skills to grow their businesses. His teaching methodology focuses on practical, real-world applications rather than just theoretical concepts.
